Goal of the Final App

This fictitious planning application is intended to introduce the idea of a specific shopping app. The main feature of this application, Location - based, is the ability to receive buzz notifications when close to a store that was previously designated using a combination of Location tracking and Geofences.

Main Goal

  1. Assembling a shopping list of products.

  2. being able to use Google Maps to find stores and bookmark their locations.

  3. obtaining the position of an app using geolocation.

  4. When an app enters a geofenced-in shop, a vibration notification is sent as a reminder.

  5. As products are purchased, check them off the shopping list.

Sub Goal

  1. User can upload profile picture/login.

  2. User can see food recipe.

The general idea is to eliminate the necessity to remind yourself to seek your shopping list once you arrive at the store.
